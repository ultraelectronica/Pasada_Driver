# Pasada Driver Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** November 8, 2025

Welcome to Pasada Driver. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you use our driver mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you consent to the practices described herein.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information from the Pasada Admin who creates your account, information you provide during app use, and information automatically gathered.

### 1.1 Information Provided by Pasada Admin

**Important:** Your driver account is created by the Pasada Admin, not through self-registration.

- **Driver Credentials:** Driver ID, full name, phone number, driver's license number, and email address (if applicable).
- **Vehicle Assignment:** Vehicle ID, plate number, and vehicle capacity.
- **Route Assignment:** Your designated route, service area, and allowed stops.

### 1.2 Information You Provide Directly

- **Login Activity:** When you log in and change your status (Online, Driving, Idling, Offline).
- **Booking Actions:** Accepting bookings, starting trips, and completing trips.
- **Support Correspondence:** Any information you send when contacting customer support.

### 1.3 Information Collected Automatically

- **Location Data:** With your permission, we continuously collect precise GPS and cellular data while you are Online, Driving, or Idling to enable passenger matching, real-time tracking, and navigation. This occurs even when the app is in the background through a foreground service with a persistent notification.

- **Trip Data:** Booking details, passenger pickup and drop-off locations, trip routes, and completion status.

- **Device & Network Information:** Device model, operating system version, unique identifiers, and network carrier.

- **Usage Analytics:** Interaction metrics (features used, service hours) to help us improve the App.

- **Local Storage:** Recent bookings, allowed stops, and encrypted credentials cached locally.

### 1.4 Information from Third Parties

- **Mapping Services:** Google Maps/Places APIs for navigation and location services.
- **Backend Services:** Supabase (driver authentication, database, real-time updates, file storage).
- **Push Notifications:** Firebase Cloud Messaging for booking alerts and system notifications.
- **Other Integrations:** Any additional vendors we engage, each subject to their own privacy practices.

## 4. Background Location Tracking

As a driver, continuous location tracking is essential to our service. We collect your location:

- While you are Online, Driving, or Idling to match you with passengers and provide real-time updates.
- In the background even when the app is not actively in use, through a foreground service with a persistent notification.
- When the app is closed or minimized during active trips to complete ongoing bookings.
- After 10 minutes of the application being in the background/recent apps, the tracking will stop and considered you as offline and not be able to recieve bookings.

**You can stop location tracking by logging out or closing the app completely from the recent apps or background.** However, you cannot accept bookings without enabling location access.
